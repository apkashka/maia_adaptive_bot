## Features
- **Adaptive difficulty** - the bot estimates your skill level from your moves and adjusts accordingly.
- **Win probability** - see your chances of winning after each move
- **Multiple starting levels** - choose your initial skill level

## How it works
The Maia-2 model predicts human-like moves at different ELO ratings. The bot analyzes your moves to estimate your strength and plays at a certain level for an enjoyable game.
